Welcome to Workout Hub

Push-ups

3 sets of 15 reps — Compound bodyweight exercise targeting chest, shoulders, and triceps. Enhances upper body endurance and stability.

Bench Press

4 sets until failure — Classic compound lift for maximal chest, shoulder, and tricep development. Focus on controlled descent and explosive press.

Dumbbell Bench Press

4 sets until failure — Promotes chest symmetry and stabilizer engagement. Allows greater range of motion than barbell press.

Cable Row

4 sets until failure — Engages mid-back and traps. Keep chest upright and pull towards lower abdomen for optimal contraction.

T-Bar Row

3 sets of 12 reps — Builds mid and upper back mass. Focus on controlled pull and full range of motion.

Lat Pulldown

4 sets until failure — Strengthens lats and upper back. Emphasize full contraction and slow eccentric control.

Reverse Fly

3 sets until failure — Strengthens rear delts and upper back. Slow, controlled movements maximize engagement.

Overhead Press

3 sets until failure — Builds overall shoulder strength and stability. Engage core for proper posture.

Arnold Press

3 sets until failure — Targets anterior and lateral delts. Full rotation engages all heads of the shoulder.

Cable Tricep Pulldown

4 sets until failure — Provides constant tension for triceps. Maintain elbow position and slow controlled movement.

Dumbbell Tricep Extension

4 sets until failure — Overhead isolation for triceps long head. Keep core tight and avoid arching lower back.

Tricep Kickback

3 sets until failure — Isolation movement to improve tricep definition. Keep elbow stationary and fully extend arm.

Plank

4 sets of 120 seconds — Static core stabilization exercise. Engage abs, glutes, and back. Maintain straight line from head to heels.

Russian Twists

4 sets of 30 reps per side — Rotational exercise activating obliques. Maintain neutral spine and slow rotation.

Bicycle Crunches

4 sets of 30 reps per side — Oblique-focused abdominal exercise. Slow controlled movement maximizes engagement.
