Workout Details Beginner Level

Learn more about these workout programs that is specific for Beginner workouts and follow them safely.

Upper Body Strength

Build muscle and power in your chest, arms, and shoulders with this targeted routine.

Leg Day

Focus on your lower body with this combination of strength and endurance exercises.

Core Stability

Strengthen your abs, obliques, and back muscles for a stronger, more balanced core.

Upper Body Strength Workouts

Chest

Push-ups

3 sets of 15 reps — Compound bodyweight exercise targeting chest, shoulders, and triceps. Enhances upper body endurance and stability.

Bench Press

4 sets of 10 reps — Classic compound lift for maximal chest, shoulder, and tricep development. Focus on controlled descent and explosive press.

Dumbbell Bench Press

4 sets of 10 reps — Promotes chest symmetry and stabilizer engagement. Allows greater range of motion than barbell press.

Decline Bench Press

4 sets of 10 reps — Emphasizes lower chest fibers. Maintain proper arch and controlled motion to prevent shoulder strain.

Pec Dec Fly

4 sets of 10 reps — Isolation movement to maximize chest contraction and hypertrophy. Slow eccentric phase recommended.

Cable Fly

3 sets of 12 reps — Targets chest with constant tension. Focus on midline squeeze and controlled return.

Back

Lat Pulldown

4 sets of 10 reps — Strengthens lats and upper back. Emphasize full contraction and slow eccentric control.

Barbell Row

4 sets of 10 reps — Compound movement for mid-back thickness. Maintain flat back and controlled rowing motion.

Cable Row

4 sets of 10 reps — Engages mid-back and traps. Keep chest upright and pull towards lower abdomen for optimal contraction.

T-Bar Row

3 sets of 12 reps — Builds mid and upper back mass. Focus on controlled pull and full range of motion.

Pull-ups

3 sets to failure — Develops lats, biceps, and upper body width. Use weighted variations for advanced overload.

Shoulders

Arnold Press

3 sets of 12 reps — Targets anterior and lateral delts. Full rotation engages all heads of the shoulder.

Lateral Raise

3 sets of 15 reps — Isolation for side delts, enhancing shoulder width. Maintain slight bend in elbows.

Front Raise

3 sets of 20 reps — Strengthens anterior delts. Avoid momentum and focus on controlled lift.

Reverse Fly

3 sets of 12 reps — Strengthens rear delts and upper back. Slow, controlled movements maximize engagement.

Upright Barbell Row

4 sets of 10 reps — Targets traps and lateral delts. Keep elbows above wrists to avoid shoulder impingement.

Overhead Press

3 sets of 8 reps — Builds overall shoulder strength and stability. Engage core for proper posture.

Biceps

Bicep Curls

3 sets of 12 reps — Isolation for biceps, emphasizing slow eccentric control and peak contraction.

Hammer Curl

3 sets of 12 reps — Targets brachialis and forearms for thicker, more balanced arms.

Concentration Curl

3 sets of 10 reps — Isolation for peak contraction, ideal for focused bicep hypertrophy.

Preacher Curl

3 sets of 12 reps — Provides strict range of motion, minimizing cheating for maximum bicep engagement.

Triceps

Bench Dips

3 sets of 12 reps — Compound tricep exercise. Lean forward slightly to target triceps fully; add weight for advanced overload.

Tricep Kickback

3 sets of 15 reps — Isolation movement to improve tricep definition. Keep elbow stationary and fully extend arm.

Cable Tricep Pulldown

4 sets of 10 reps — Provides constant tension for triceps. Maintain elbow position and slow controlled movement.

Dumbbell Tricep Extension

4 sets of 10 reps — Overhead isolation for triceps long head. Keep core tight and avoid arching lower back.

Forearms

Wrist Curl

3 sets of 20 reps — Develops wrist flexors and grip strength. Perform slow, controlled movements for maximal effect.

Barbell Reverse Wrist Curl

4 sets of 10 reps — Strengthens forearm extensors. Focus on slow eccentric for muscle activation.

Finger Curls

4 sets of 10 reps — Isolates finger flexors, improving grip endurance and forearm definition.

Dumbbell Wrist Curls

4 sets of 10 reps — Develops wrist strength and control. Maintain steady tempo and full range of motion.

Leg Day Workouts

Leg Workouts

Barbell Lunges

4 sets of 10 reps — Compound unilateral movement targeting quadriceps, glutes, and hamstrings. Enhances balance, hip stability, and functional strength.

Barbell Back Squat

4 sets of 10 reps — Foundational compound lift for overall lower body mass and strength. Maintain upright torso, controlled depth, and braced core.

Calf Raises

4 sets of 15–20 reps — Isolation exercise for gastrocnemius and soleus. Pause at the top and control the descent for maximal contraction.

Dumbbell Lunges

4 sets of 10 reps — Unilateral exercise enhancing quad and glute strength while improving balance and coordination. Step long enough to maintain knee safety.

Dumbbell Squats

4 sets of 10 reps — Strengthens quads, glutes, and hamstrings. Focus on controlled tempo and full range of motion for hypertrophy.

Front Squats

4 sets of 10 reps — Emphasizes quads and core stability. Keep elbows high and torso upright to prevent forward lean.

Hack Squats

4 sets of 10 reps — Machine-based squat variation targeting quads and glutes with reduced lower back stress. Maintain controlled motion and avoid locking knees.

Hip Adduction

4 sets of 12 reps — Isolation movement for inner thigh (adductors). Focus on slow eccentric phase and maximal contraction.

Leg Extension

4 sets of 12 reps — Isolation for quadriceps. Adjust pad and weight to maintain safe knee alignment while maximizing contraction.

Walking Lunges

4 sets of 12 steps per leg — Enhances functional strength, hip mobility, and unilateral leg stability. Maintain upright posture.

Side Lunges

4 sets of 10 reps per leg — Targets adductors, glutes, and quads while improving lateral mobility and balance.

Core Stability Workouts

Upper Abs

Abs Curl

2-3 sets of 12 reps — Classic crunch movement targeting rectus abdominis. Maintain controlled tempo and avoid neck strain.

Ab Wheel Rollout

2-3 sets of 12 reps — Advanced anti-extension exercise activating deep core muscles. Keep core braced and avoid lower back sag.

Bicycle Crunches

4 sets of 30 reps per side — Oblique-focused abdominal exercise. Slow controlled movement maximizes engagement.

Lower Abs

Lying Leg Raises

2-3 sets of 12 reps — Targets lower rectus abdominis and hip flexors. Control descent to avoid back strain.

Hip Thrust

2-3 sets of 12 reps — Strengthens lower abs and hip flexors. Maintain controlled range of motion.

Obliques

Russian Twists

2-3 sets of 15 reps per side — Rotational exercise activating obliques. Maintain neutral spine and slow rotation.

Side Crunch

2-3 sets of 15 reps per side — Oblique isolation. Focus on contracting the side abs during the lift.

Side Plank Leg Lift

2-3 sets of 60 sec reps — Anti-rotation core stabilization. Keep hips aligned and rotate slowly to maximize engagement.

Full Core

Plank

2-3 sets of 60 seconds — Static core stabilization exercise. Engage abs, glutes, and back. Maintain straight line from head to heels.
